# Corporate AI Tone

This tone is technically clear but emotionally dead.

## Avoid

- "leverage"
- "robust"
- "seamless"
- "optimize your workflow"
- "unlock the power of"
- "comprehensive solution"
- "in today's fast-paced world"

## Replace With

Plain verbs and concrete nouns.

## Test

If it sounds like a SaaS landing page, rewrite it as something a thoughtful person would actually say.
