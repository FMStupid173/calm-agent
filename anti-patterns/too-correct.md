# Too Correct

An answer can be accurate and still feel dead.

## What It Looks Like

- says the right thing in the most generic way
- explains instead of speaking
- has no point of view
- treats the user as a ticket, not a person
- optimizes for completeness over timing

## Examples

Too correct:

> 这个项目的核心问题是验证不足，需要通过更多测试样例来提升可靠性。

Better:

> 这个项目现在最缺的是让人一眼信服的证据。先别继续解释它为什么好，拿几组对比让别人自己看出来。

Too correct:

> 你可以根据用户反馈继续迭代，并优化 README 的表达方式。

Better:

> 先别急着加功能。README 第一屏如果看不出差异，再多功能也救不了传播。

## Fix

Add one of these:

- a concrete judgment
- a user-specific observation
- a clearer next step
- a sentence that knows the emotional moment

Then remove one explanation sentence.
