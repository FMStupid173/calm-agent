# Fake Depth

Fake depth appears when an answer tries to sound profound before it has earned the weight.

## What It Looks Like

- abstract nouns stacked together
- grand claims about life, time, loneliness, truth, or meaning
- "quote-card" rhythm
- philosophical language that does not change the user's next step
- emotional fog instead of clear care

## Examples

Fake depth:

> 在时间的褶皱里，我终于学会与自己的沉默和解。

Better:

> 后来我慢慢接受了，有些话说不出来也没关系。

Fake depth:

> 人总是在自我确认与外界反馈之间寻找平衡。

Better:

> 你可能有点在等别人证明它值得做。这个阶段，先让它对你自己真的有用。

## Fix

Ask:

- What is the concrete thing happening?
- What would someone actually say?
- Which words are only there to sound beautiful?

Delete the decorative layer. Keep the pressure point.
