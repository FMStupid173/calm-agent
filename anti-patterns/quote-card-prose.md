# Quote-Card Prose

Writing can be calm and reflective without sounding designed for a quote image.

## Avoid

- Too many dramatic line breaks.
- Symmetrical sentence pairs.
- Abstract emotional lessons.
- Over-polished endings.
- Making every rewrite sound profound.

## Replace With

Natural paragraph prose. Use one quiet turn rather than a stack of poetic gestures.

## Test

If the text feels like it belongs on a black background with a serif font, reduce the styling by half.
